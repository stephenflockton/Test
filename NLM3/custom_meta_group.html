  • NLM 3.0

    Documentation for the IOP Publishing specific implementation of NLM 3.0

    • Root Element

      The root element for IOP Journals is <article>

    • Front

      Container element for the journal and article metadata

      • Journal Meta

        Values for elements in <journal-meta> can be found in the journal dossier

      • Article Meta

        Capture metadata about the article in the <article-meta> element.

        • Article Identifiers

          Capture article identifers in <article-id> elements

        • Article Categories

          Capture article type and any subject section/special issue using article-categories

        • Article Title

          Capture article titles in a <article-title> element contained in a <title-group> element

        • Contrib-group

          Capture authors and affiliations using <contrib-group>

        • Author Notes

          Any author footnotes are tagged with <author-notes>.

        • Publication dates

          <pub-date> is used to capture publication dates

        • Volume

          The volume of the journal in which the article is published

        • Issue

          The issue of the journal in which the article is published

        • Article Number

          The article number of the article within the journal issue

        • Page Numbers

          The page numbers of the article within the journal issue

        • Supplementary Material

          Use the <supplementary-material> element within <article-meta> as a flag for the presence of features that are not available in all versions of the article.

        • History

          Container element for dates not relating to publication of the article.

        • Copyright

          Capture the article copyright details in the <permission> element.

        • License

          Capture the <license-p> element in the <license> element.

        • Abstract

          Capture the abstract text in the <abstract> element.

        • Keyword group

          Capture the <kwd> elements in <kwd-group> with a @kwd-group-type

        • Funding group

          Capture all funding data in one <funding-group> element

        • Counts

          Capture the page count in <counts>

        • Custom meta group

          Capture all the <custom-meta> elements in <custom-meta-group>

    • Body

      Capture the main text of the article in the <body> element

    • Back

      Capture the back matter in the <back> element.
