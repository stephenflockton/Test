Article Type

All articles must have a @article-type attribute on the root <article> element

/article[@article-type]
The allowed values of article-type are:
  • research-article
  • rapid-communication
  • review-article
  • article-commentary
  • reply
  • correction
  • addendum
  • editorial
  • introduction
  • announcement
  • note
  • discussion
  • article-commentary
