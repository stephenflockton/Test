Acknowledgements

Capture any acknowledgements in the <ack> element

/article/back/ack

Capture the acknowledgement title in <title>. This is “Acknowledgements” unless specified otherwise. Do no capture a <label> child of <ack>.

Any funding data which makes up part of the text in <p> in <ack> must also capture in the article metadata in <funding-group>.
