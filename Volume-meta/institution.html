institution

Capture the institution details in the <institution> element within <conference-location>

/volume-meta/conference/conference-location/institution

The name of the institution where the <conference> was held is captured in <institution>. The institution's address is not captured.

1

<conference-location>
  <institution>University of Greenwich</institution>
  <city>London</city>
  <country>United Kingdom</country>
</conference-location>
