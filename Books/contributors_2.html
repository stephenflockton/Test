Contributors

Contributor information for a book is captured in <contrib-group> elements.

Information about the book authors, editors, collaboration groups is captured in a contrib-group element. This element must also contain the affliation data, contributor footnotes and biographies. See the following sections for specific instructions.

Example

<contrib-group>
  <contrib contrib-type="author">
    <name name-style="western">
      <surname>McGurn</surname>
      <given-names>Arthur R</given-names>
    </name>
    <xref ref-type="aff" rid="aff1"/>
    <xref ref-type="bio" rid="bio1"/>
  </contrib>
<aff id="aff1">Department of Physics, <institution>Western Michigan
University</institution>, Kalamazoo, MI, <country>USA</country>
</aff>
<bio id="bio1">
    <p>Arthur McGurn is Professor Emeritus of Physics at Western Michigan
  University. He received his PhD from the University of California,
  Santa Barbara in 1975, working in theoretical condensed matter
  physics. He is a Fellow of the Institute of Physics, the American
  Physical Society, the Electromagnetics Academy, and the Optical
  Society of America, and is an Outstanding Referee of the American
  Physical Society. He has over 150 research publications.</p>
  </bio>
</contrib-group>
